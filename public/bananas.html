Bananas

Shopping list chaos for you and your significant other? Bananas is the answer. It's the impossibly simple shared shopping list just for couples!
Simple:
  • 1. connect in person via BlueTooth (you'll stay in sync while you're apart).
  • 2. add items to the list - your partner will be instantly notified.
  • 3. check off items as you shop.
    • No registration required, no fussy usernames or passwords.
    Just ONE list to manage for both of you.
    That's it!
